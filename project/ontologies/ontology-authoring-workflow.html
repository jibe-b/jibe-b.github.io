RDF Graph editing (ABox)

Goals:

  • store the rules (Physical Sciences knowledge) that are embedded into the axioms,
  • reference of knowledge theoretical objects (theories and models), knowledge propositions (theorems) and their explicit form, using the ontology.

Ontology refining (TBox)

Iterative process:

  • extraction of results from the graph authoring and embedding into ontology
  • saving of OWL reasoners results as axioms
  • writing of these results in the RDF graph
  • traduction of SWRL reasoning results into OWL rules when possible
  • refining of the RDF Graph
