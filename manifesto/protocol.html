Protocol

My principles

  • I aim to produce knowledge — valid in the indicated intervals
  • my work aims to be reproducible

Our relation

  • what I produce is a Common, accessible and under a free licence
  • I invite you to judge by yourself the rigor of the statements and the reproducibility of the work

My personal motivations

  • as a person, I want to make knowledge available to humankind
  • provide ways to see things from a different point of view
  • give a place to all opinions as long as debate rules are respected

Cooperation

  • even though one should manage to achieve basics goals with success alone, I believe that great things can only be achieved through sharing
  • cooperation can be achieved through sharing of energy, ideas, synchronisation of action and debate
  • coooperation is also an end in itself, enabling ideas to be living, intereacting, possibly merging or transformed to more complex ideas

Concretely

Under certain delay, we can collaborate

  • interaction, discussion takes no time, write me an email whenever you have the time to do so or come to my office on Friday (open Büro)
  • discussion, debate, synchronisation takes some time, so contact me early, and let us maturate the idea together
  • action may be short or long, as long as we respect this protocol, we should manage to do something together
